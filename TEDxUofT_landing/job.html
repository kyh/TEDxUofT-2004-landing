Executive assistant

Posted on October 29th

If you are the friend everyone flocks for your notes because they are actually better than the professor’s we want you to apply. This is easily the most urgent and important position we are looking to add to our team, and given its dynamic nature you’ll be able to work in various aspects of the conference.


As the executive assistant you’ll provide support as needed to the executive team in particular to (project management and logistics), but you’ll be responsible for:


  • Managing the schedules of the executives from 3 different campuses and commitmens in order to find meeting times.
  • Work with project manager to create meeting agendas.
  • Assist project manager in collecting updates from team members.
  • Maintain a master task lists and the priority of tasks in terms of deadlines.
  • Take meeting minutes and upload them after each team meeting.
  • Assign tasks and deadlines to executives based on meeting content.
